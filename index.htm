ABOUT US

NOLBU

"We are the one spot on Convoy that isn't... Convoy."

In San Diego, you’ll find two distinctive cuisines - the local Mexican of the south, and the Asian American food of migrants from near and far.

Within Nolbu, you’ll find a marriage of both contained in the humble establishment. Backed by a motley collection of exotic beers, cocktails, and other spirits curated by owners Pat and Bause #2, the gastropub has earned the affection of locals and visitors alike.

Having only been around for a year, Nolbu has already established itself as one of Convoy’s most unique and lovable junctions.

"WE ARE SAN DIEGO, and we have the San Diego lifestyle down pat. We’re small, so we’re intimate, but large enough that we can offer something for everyone. We’re doing something that encapsulates San Diego, not Convoy."
